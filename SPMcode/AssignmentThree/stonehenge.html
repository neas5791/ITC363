User interface

Keyboard interaction:

  • W and S move the viewer respectively forwards and backwards along the view direction in 1 metre steps.
  • A and D move the viewer respectively left and right perpendicular to an unchanging view direction in 1 metre steps. This is unusual cradwise motion, but easy to implement.
  • Q and E swing the view direction respectively left and right in 2 degree increments, without changing the eye position.

Button interaction:

Just for fun I have included adjustment of the vertical field of view, initially set at 27 degrees, corresponding roughly to a standard 50mm SLR lens. It can be increased or decreased in steps of 6 degrees to a maximum of 45 degrees (approx 30mm SLR lens) and a minimum of 15 degrees (approx 90mm SLR lens).
