Construction of Polygon

Move the slider at the bottom of this frame to change the number of vertices your polygon will have. To construct the polygon simply click in the canvas area to the left; the selected points will form the vertices of the polygon. During this selection process the lines representing the edges formed by the vertices will be drawn, if the edge breaches either condition of winding or intersection the line will be drawn in RED colour. If a RED line appears an appropriate error message will be displayed. At the completion of vertex selection the polygon will render in a solid BLUE colour.The polygon construction process can be started again at anytime by pressing the escape (esc) key or changing the number of vertices.

Navigation and Manipultaion

The below controls buttons (keyboard short keys shown in brackets) will translate the complete polygon relative to the viewing window up(w), down(s), left(a) and right(d). The polygon can also be rotated about it's centre using the relative rotation buttons or the (q) and (e) keys. The target button (x) in the centre of the navigation controls translates the polygon to the canvas [0,0] position.
